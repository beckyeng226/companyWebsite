Our Mission

At Capability Apps, we strive to maximize independence for all individuals by developing customized assitive and adaptive technology.

Our Products

Most of our products are custom-designed for the individual needs of the users. Because our products are meant to help individuals live more independently within their environments, we consult with all stakeholders, including family, caretakers, educators, therapists, and employers, to identify what needs the app will address and how to best accomplish that for the users.
A few examples of products that have previously been developed:

    Educational Apps

  • Apps to teach touchscreen skills/cause and effect as a precursor for touchscreen communication devices

    Employment-support Apps

  • Calculator app with photos of actual currency to help in determining correct change

    Social Interaction Apps

  • Turn-taking games
